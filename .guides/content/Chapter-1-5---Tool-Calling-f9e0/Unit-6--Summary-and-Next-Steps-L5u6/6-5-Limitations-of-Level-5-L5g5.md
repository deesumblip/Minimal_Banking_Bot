**Starting point:** **`level5/`** with your Level 5 agent.

## What Level 5 does not cover

You still have **one** main assistant with a **`tools/`** module. **Sub-agents** (delegate a whole task to another agent, MCP, handback) are **Level 6**.

Advanced tool chaining, custom validation frameworks, and production routing are out of scope here—you extend the same patterns when you need them.

## When to move on

Choose **Level 6** when you need **orchestration** across agents. **Level 6 Unit 0** explains how **`level6/`** extends **`level5/`**—read **0-1** / **0-2** for that story in full.
