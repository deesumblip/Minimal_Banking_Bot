**Starting point:** **`level5/`** with your Chapter 1.5 agent.

## What Chapter 1.5 does not cover

You still have **one** main assistant with a **`tools/`** module. **Sub-agents** (delegate a whole task to another agent, MCP, handback) are **Chapter 1.6**.

Advanced tool chaining, custom validation frameworks, and production routing are out of scope here—you extend the same patterns when you need them.

## When to move on

Choose **Chapter 1.6** when you need **orchestration** across agents. **Chapter 1.6 Unit 0** explains how **`level6/`** extends **`level5/`**—read **0-1** / **0-2** for that story in full.
