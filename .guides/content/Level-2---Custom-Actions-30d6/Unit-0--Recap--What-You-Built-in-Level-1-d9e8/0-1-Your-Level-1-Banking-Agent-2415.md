## What You Built in Level 1

Your Level 1 agent had just responses. The agent couldn't check the current date, query a database, or return anything that varies at runtime.

---

## What Level 2 Adds

Level 2 introduces actions: Python classes the action server executes during a conversation. Your Level 1 files remain unchanged. Level 2 adds new files on top of them.

The `actions/` folder already exists in your starter project, including an example action (`actions/action_bank_hours.py`) you will study in Units 2 and 3. You will build `action_holiday_hours.py` yourself.

| Lab | What you do |
|---|---|
| Lab 3.1 | Write `action_holiday_hours.py` |
| Lab 4.1 | Register both actions in `domain/basics.yml` under `actions:` |
| Lab 5.1 | Create `data/basics/hours.yml` and `data/basics/holiday_hours.yml` |

Many of the Level 1 responses, flows, and configuration files carry forward without modification.
