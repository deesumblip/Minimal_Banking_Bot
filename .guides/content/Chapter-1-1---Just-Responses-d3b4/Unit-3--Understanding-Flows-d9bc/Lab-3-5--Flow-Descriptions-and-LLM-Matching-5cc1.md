#### Why these flows?

**Section 3.4 Flow Descriptions and LLM Understanding** explains why **`description`** text matters. The LLM uses it to pick the right flow when users ask things like “What are your hours?” or “How do I check my balance?” In this lab you practice that idea on two skeleton flows named **hours** and **balance**. After you pass both fill-in-the-blank checks here, **Lab 3.6** walks you through copying the finished YAML into your project and adding the matching responses.
