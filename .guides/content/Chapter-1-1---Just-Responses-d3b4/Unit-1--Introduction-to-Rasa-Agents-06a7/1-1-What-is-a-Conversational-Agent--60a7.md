
A **conversational agent** is a computer program that can have natural, human-like conversations with users. Unlike traditional programs that require specific commands, conversational agents understand natural language and respond in kind.

#### Key Characteristics

1. **Natural Language Understanding (NLU)**: The agent can understand what users mean, even when they phrase things differently. For example:
   - "What are your hours?"
   - "When are you open?"
   - "Tell me your operating hours"
   
   All three mean the same thing, and a good agent understands them all.

2. **Context Awareness**: Advanced agents can remember previous parts of the conversation. (Note: Level 1 agents don't have memory yet - that comes in Level 3.)

3. **Flow-Based Logic**: The agent follows predefined conversation paths (called "flows") that determine what happens next.

#### Types of Agents

- **Rule-Based Agents**: Follow strict if-then rules. Limited flexibility.
- **AI-Powered Agents**: Use machine learning to understand intent and respond naturally. This is what Rasa Pro provides.
