# Lab 7.2: Build Your Own Feature (Project)

**Objective**: In earlier units you saw how to add responses in Unit 2 and Lab 2.2, and flows in Unit 3 and Labs 3.2–3.5. In this lab you create your own feature: one new response in `domain/basics.yml` and one new flow in `data/basics/` that uses it. Choose something simple, such as branch locations or an FAQ. Use clear flow names and descriptions. Test in Inspector.

**Why this lab**: So far you've followed step-by-step instructions. Here you apply the same pattern on your own: one response, one flow, a clear description. That proves you can extend the agent without a handout and gives you a template for adding more features later. The assessment checks that you added a new flow, not just the ones from earlier labs, and that the domain and flow are correctly linked.
